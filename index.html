Welcome to FoodSave

Connecting restaurants with surplus meals to budget-conscious diners.

Features

Real-Time Food Listing Dashboard:

Restaurant staff can instantly upload available surplus items with photos, ingredients, allergens, quantity, original price, and discounted price.

Flexible Discount Pricing Controls:

Restaurants set dynamic discount percentages (30-70% off) by meal category and time window.

Pickup Time Windows:

Restaurants define specific pickup windows to concentrate customer traffic and simplify logistics.

Merchant Rating & Reliability System:

Restaurants earn ratings based on food quality, accuracy, timeliness, and freshness.

Secure Payment & Reservation System:

Customers pay via app before pickup to reserve meals and guarantee sale for restaurants.

Allergen & Dietary Preference Filters:

Advanced tagging system allows restaurants to label meals by diet type and allergens.

Impact Tracking Dashboard:

Displays environmental and financial metrics for both restaurants and customers.

Geolocation & Distance Filtering:

Map-based interface shows nearby restaurants with real-time availability.

Restaurant Analytics & Insights:

Weekly reports show meal types selling fastest and optimal discount thresholds.

Loyalty & Referral Program:

Customers earn points per purchase redeemable for discounts or free meals.
