Features

Real-Time Food Listing Dashboard

Restaurants post available surplus items with photos, portion sizes, and expiration times. Listings go live immediately and push notifications alert nearby customers within configurable radius.

Dynamic Discount Pricing Controls

Restaurant owners set tiered discounts (30%, 50%, 70% off) based on time remaining and inventory levels. Automated pricing adjustments preserve brand margins while clearing stock.

Pickup Time Window Scheduling

Restaurants define specific pickup windows when surplus items are ready. Customers reserve items with confirmed pickup times, ensuring better inventory management.

Integrated Rating and Review System

Customers and restaurants can rate each other to maintain trust and accountability. Ratings visible on profiles help in making informed decisions.

Secure Payment and Redemption

In-app payment processing with digital receipts and QR code verification at pickup ensures a seamless experience for customers and restaurants.
