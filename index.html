Connecting Restaurants with Budget-Conscious Customers

Quality Meals at Unbeatable Discounts - Reduce Waste, Save Money

Features

Restaurant Dashboard - Food Listing Interface

Allows restaurants to upload end-of-day unsold items with high-quality photos, original prices, discounted prices (50-70% off), item descriptions, quantity available, and pickup time windows (typically 30-60 minutes before closing).

Real-Time Inventory Availability

Displays live stock levels on customer app with automatic deactivation when items sell out or reach pickup deadline, preventing overselling and customer disappointment.

Location-Based Search & Filtering

Enables customers to find restaurants within 2-5 km radius, filter by cuisine type, discount percentage, and dietary preferences, with maps integration for navigation.

In-App Payment Processing

Secure payment gateway accepting credit cards, digital wallets, and stored payment methods with instant order confirmation, reducing friction at pickup.

Push Notifications & Alerts

Real-time alerts when favorite restaurants list items, inventory about to expire, or new restaurants join in customer's area, driving immediate engagement and conversions.

Restaurant Analytics Dashboard

Provides restaurants with insights on waste reduction metrics, revenue recovered, popular items, peak discount times, and customer demand patterns to optimize future listings.

Customer Ratings & Reviews

Allows customers to review individual food items and restaurants, building trust and helping restaurants understand quality feedback on discounted offerings.

Pickup Time Management

Enforces strict pickup windows with countdown timers, allows customers to select preferred pickup slots, and enables restaurants to hold orders with automated reminders.

Social Impact Tracking

Shows customers and restaurants aggregated food waste prevented in kilograms, CO2 emissions saved, and meals rescued, creating positive engagement loop and shareable impact metrics.

Restaurant Verification & Compliance

Implements food safety certification verification, health inspection records display, and allergen/dietary information management to ensure customer trust and regulatory compliance.
