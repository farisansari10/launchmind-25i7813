Welcome to Surplus Eats

A mobile marketplace connecting cost-conscious diners with delicious surplus meals.

Features

Real-Time Inventory Listing

Restaurants can instantly upload available surplus meals with photos, descriptions, and dynamic pricing.

Dynamic Discount Pricing

Automatic price reduction for items approaching end-of-day, with percentage discounts based on time remaining.

In-App Reservation System

Customers can reserve and pre-pay for surplus meals, guaranteeing pickup and reducing no-shows.

Geolocation Meal Discovery

Show nearby restaurants with available surplus meals within user's proximity.

Waste Reduction Analytics

Provide restaurants detailed reports on food saved, money recovered, and environmental impact.
