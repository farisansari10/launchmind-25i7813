Reduce Food Waste & Save Money

FoodSave connects restaurants with budget-conscious customers for real-time discounts on unsold inventory.

Key Features

Restaurant Inventory Listing Dashboard

Intuitive mobile and desktop interface allowing restaurants to photograph, categorize, describe, and set discounted prices for unsold dishes 30-120 minutes before closing; includes prep time, allergen tags, and portion size specifications.

Real-Time Inventory Synchronization

Automatic updates to customer-facing listings as dishes are reserved or sold, preventing overselling; uses QR codes or barcode scanning at restaurant point-of-sale to track quantities and immediately reflect availability changes.

Dynamic Discount Pricing Control

Restaurant-controlled pricing rules allowing owners to set base discounts (50-70%) per item, adjust in real-time based on time-to-close and inventory levels, and apply bulk discounts for customers purchasing multiple items in single transactions.

Customer Browse and Search Interface

Location-based restaurant discovery with filters for cuisine type, discount percentage, pickup time windows, dietary restrictions, and ratings; displays high-quality food photos, exact discount applied, and countdown timers to restaurant closing.

Pickup Scheduling and Reservation System

Customers select items, reserve quantities with guaranteed availability, choose 15-30 minute pickup windows before restaurant closing, and receive notifications when order is ready for collection; restaurants confirm order fulfillment status.

Bi-Directional Rating and Review System

Customers rate food quality, freshness, and value after pickup; restaurants rate customer reliability and punctuality; transparent feedback builds trust and identifies quality compliance issues for restaurant operators.

Push Notification and Alert System

Customers receive notifications when nearby restaurants list new items, discount thresholds they set are met, or pickup windows are opening; restaurants receive alerts when items are claimed and reminders for pickup times.

Restaurant Analytics Dashboard

Real-time metrics showing daily waste percentage reduction, revenue recovered from unsold inventory, most-claimed dishes, customer acquisition cost, and historical trends to guide future pricing and preparation strategies.

Secure Payment Processing

In-app payment through credit/debit cards or digital wallets, with pre-ordering and prepayment required to reduce no-shows; PCI-compliant transactions with split payment support for multi-item orders from same restaurant.

Restaurant Onboarding and Compliance Tools

Guided setup for restaurant operations including food category templates, health code compliance documentation, operational guidelines for discount limits and quality standards, and training materials for staff on platform usage.
