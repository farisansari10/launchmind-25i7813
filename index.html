FoodRescue: Nourishing People, Saving Food

Connecting restaurants with surplus meals to budget-conscious diners for meaningful savings and sustainable choices.

Our Features

Restaurant Surplus Listing Dashboard

Real-time admin panel allowing restaurants to upload surplus dishes with high-quality photos, exact pricing (50-70% off regular menu), portion quantities, allergen/dietary tags, and precise pickup windows (e.g., 8:30-9:15 PM). Includes bulk upload templates for high-volume restaurants.

Customer Discovery & Filtering App

Location-based feed showing nearby available surplus meals within 2km radius with filtering by cuisine type, dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, halal), price range, restaurant rating, and pickup time availability. Includes push notifications for new listings from favorited restaurants.

Reservation & Pickup Management System

One-tap meal reservation with confirmation codes, real-time pickup status updates, in-app chat between customer and restaurant staff, and automatic cancellation if meals unclaimed after pickup window. QR code scanning at restaurant for verification.

Dual-Sided Rating & Review System

Five-star ratings with detailed reviews for restaurants (food quality, portion accuracy, freshness, staff friendliness) and customers (reliable pickups, communication, respectfulness). Flagging system for quality violations with temporary listing suspension at 3+ violations.

Analytics Dashboard for Restaurants

Restaurants view weekly waste reduction metrics (kg saved, CO2 prevented), revenue recovered from surplus sales, customer demographics, peak listing times, and popular dishes. Monthly sustainability report exportable for marketing/CSR purposes.
