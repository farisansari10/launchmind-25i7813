Connecting Consumers with Surplus Meals

A mobile platform to reduce food waste while offering affordable dining options.

Features

Real-Time Food Inventory Listing

Restaurant dashboard allowing instant uploading of surplus meals with current quantity, original price, and discounted price.

Dynamic Pricing Algorithm

Automatic discount calculation based on food type, time of day, remaining quantity, and historical sales data.

Geolocation-Based Meal Discovery

Mobile app feature showing nearby restaurants with available surplus meals within user's proximity.

Contactless Purchase and Pickup

Digital ordering system with QR code redemption and scheduled pickup windows for purchased surplus meals.

Sustainability Impact Tracking

User dashboard showing personal food waste reduction metrics and environmental impact from surplus meal purchases.
