Discover Delicious Deals on Surplus Food!

Connecting budget-conscious consumers with restaurants offering steep discounts on surplus food.

Features

Real-Time Inventory Management

Automated tracking of restaurant surplus food with minute-by-minute updates on available items, quantities, and discounts.

Dynamic Pricing Algorithm

Machine learning-powered pricing system that adjusts discounts based on item type, time of day, and historical sell-through rates.

Restaurant Onboarding Portal

Streamlined digital platform for restaurants to quickly upload inventory, set pricing rules, and manage listings with minimal friction.

User Rating and Trust System

Bidirectional rating mechanism allowing customers and restaurants to rate each transaction, building community trust and accountability.

Geolocation-Based Pickup/Delivery Logistics

Integrated mapping system enabling users to filter nearby restaurants, schedule pickups, and optionally request third-party delivery.
