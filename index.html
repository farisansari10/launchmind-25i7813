Transforming Surplus into Savings

Connecting restaurants with unsold inventory to budget-conscious diners at unbeatable prices

Features

  • Restaurant Inventory Listing Dashboard: Simple admin panel allowing restaurants to upload end-of-day food items with high-resolution photos, item descriptions, quantity available, original price, discounted price (50-70% off), and pickup time window.
  • Real-time Geolocation Discovery Map: Customer-facing map showing nearby restaurants with available discounted meals, filterable by cuisine type, distance, discount percentage, and pickup time.
  • Meal Detail & Reserve System: Detailed product pages showing restaurant name, item photo, original price, discount price, quantity remaining, exact pickup time window, and dietary tags.
  • Restaurant Analytics & Waste Tracking: Dashboard displaying daily waste reduction metrics, revenue recovered from discounted sales, items listed vs. items sold, and monthly reports on environmental impact.
  • Customer Review & Rating System: Post-pickup reviews allowing customers to rate meal quality, freshness, and accuracy to incentivize quality listings by restaurants.
  • Payment & Pickup Verification: Integrated payment processing with digital receipt generation and QR code scanning at pickup for verification.
  • Push Notification System: Smart notifications alerting customers about new items, pickup window approaching, and high-discount items matching saved preferences.
  • Restaurant Onboarding & Training: Video tutorials and customer support for restaurants to manage daily listings efficiently.
  • Social Impact Dashboard: Public-facing metrics showing total meals saved and sustainability commitment shareable on social media.
